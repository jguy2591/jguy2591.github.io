Mission Statement

We are a covenant people of God, a new community under the headship of Jesus Christ. We aim to make disciples, equipping the saints for the work of the ministry. We are Bible centered, church centered and mission centered. We represent a part of the multi-ethnic community of believers who belong to Christ and His kingdom. What we stand for: We hold a belief of Lordship and sovereign reign of Christ over His church as well as the whole domain of our human existence (absolute Lordship of Christ over all areas of life), which will result in transforming modern culture to Christâ€™s culture and be transformed by the world. Although no doctrine is perfect, we stand with the doctrines summarized in the Bible by the Westminster Confession of Faith-Shorter and Larger Catechism. We hold to reformed faith; the Bible is the final authority and rule of life for believers. We believe in infallibility and inerrancy of the Scripture.
